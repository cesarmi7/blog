THINGS TO KNOW

Basic Travel Information

Languages: Spanish. English is partially spoken, as well as Quechua and Aymara.

Currency: El Nuevo Sol (Soles/PEN).

Altitude: Depends on the city, but if you're planing to do the Inca Trail, you need to prepare your body for the high altitude.

Plugs: Type A and Type C. Standard frequency is 60 Hz, and standard voltage is 220 V.

Safety: You can generally expect a safe experience in Peru.

BEST TIME TO VISIT PERU

Dry and rainy season

May to October: The dry season, is when you should try to visit Machu Picchu and the Andes. While there will be more people and relatively greater prices during this peak time, the trekking conditions will be better, along with clear skies and pleasant temperatures.

November to April: This is the rainy season. This creates difficult trekking conditions because the routes are sometimes unexpectedly closed by landfalls and are frequently wet and slippery. February is also a closed month for the Inca Trail.
